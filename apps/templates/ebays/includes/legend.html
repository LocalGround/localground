Particulate Matter Concentrations

Good: less than 50 µg/m³
Moderate: between 50 and 99 µg/m³
Unhealthy for Some: between 100 and 150 µ/m³
Unhealthy: between 150 and 199 µ/m³
Very Unhealthy: between 200 and 300 µ/m³
Hazardous: more than 300 µ/m³
